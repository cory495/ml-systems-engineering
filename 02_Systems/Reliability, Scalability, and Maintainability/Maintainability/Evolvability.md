# Evolvability

Date: 2026-06-05

---

## 1. Definition

---
Evolvability is the ease at which changes can be made.
## 2. Why It Matters

---
Systems do not remain the same.
## 3. Key Ideas

---
- Systems do not remain the same.
- Allowing systems to be easily changed is important.
## 4. Examples

---
Minecraft has had over 20 large updates. Evolvability is key in this project.
## 5. How It Is Achieved

---
Agile techniques are useful for achieving this.
- Test-driven development
- Refactoring
## 6. Tradeoffs

---
- Evolvability can have a trade off in regards to time because making systems evolvable involves an investment in time.
