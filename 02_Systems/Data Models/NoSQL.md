# NoSQL

Date: 2026-06-07

---

## 1. Definition

---
NoSQL stands for not only SQL. It does not refer to any specific technology.
## 2. Why It Matters

---
NoSQL was the latest attempt to overthrow SQL.
## 3. Key Ideas

---
The driving forces behind NoSQL are:
- A need for greater scalability.
- A preference of free and open source software.
- Specialized query operations unsupported by the relational model.
- Frustration with restrictiveness of relational schemas.
## 4. Examples

---
Any non-relational data model is considered to be NoSQL
## 5. Tradeoffs

---
Moving away from SQL to NoSQL can give up the pros of SQL, like high data integrity.
