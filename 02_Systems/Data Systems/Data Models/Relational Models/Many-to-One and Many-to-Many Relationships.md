# Many-to-One and Many-to-Many Relationships

Date: 2026-06-07

---

## 1. Definition

---
Many-to-one relationships are when there are many objects with a link to one object. Many-to-many relationships are when there are many objects with links to many other objects.
## 2. Why It Matters

---
This changes how the database is set up. For relational data models, it is typically sensible to create a new table with IDs and names for one-to-many relationships.
## 3. Key Ideas

---
- Relational models are better than document models for this particular relationship.
## 4. Examples

---
- Many people go to one school.
- May people go to multiple schools over their academic career.
- Many people work at one company.
